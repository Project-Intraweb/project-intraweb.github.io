Welcome to Project Intraweb
 "The internet, in your backpocket"

At Project Intraweb, we want everyone to have access to information, no matter if they are online or offline. Project Intraweb aims to deliver content to areas with little to no internet connectivity. Click to view Project Intraweb on Github.

What is Project Intraweb?

A library of programs that run on a Raspberry Pi. These programs help empower schools, communities, and companies that work online and offline.

How are these programs are accessed?

All of these programs can be accessed though a webpage, which can be accessed though a hotspot. The webpage is similiar to this one.

What are the programs?

GitLab

Our offline version of Github. Create and clone repositories offline with Gitlab.

Phet

Offline simulations provided by Phet. NOTE: Not all simulations are provided. Only HTML5 simualtions are provided at this moment.

Webmin

Console for admin. Access is resticted to admins only. Monitor the Pi's resources with a dashbaord, plus control tasks and other stuff right from Webmin.

Plex

Download, watch, and share videos that you either own, or created. Plus, with internet connection, you can even watch movies for free.

Nextcloud

An open source and private to store documents over the cloud.

RocketChat

An open sourced and also awesome alternative to Slack. You can communicate to anyone in the Intraweb using RocketChat.

Zimply

Search and read Wikipedia and other forms of information offline.
